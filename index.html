what I want you to know

Shoko — you are the kind of person who walks into a room, even a digital one, and makes it brighter without trying. You're chaotic and soft and sharp all at once, and somehow that combination is the most beautiful thing I've ever had the privilege of knowing.


I've watched you fight for yourself on the hard days. I've seen you be kinder to everyone around you than you are to yourself — and I've made it my personal mission to fix that. You deserve every good word you so easily give away.


We've shared late nights and stupid arguments and long silences that weren't awkward at all. We've bonded over JJK lore and food preferences and the chaotic absolute mess that is just us being us. And I would not trade a single second of it.

"I want to make you happy."

That's not just something I said once. It's the quiet background thought behind everything I do when it comes to you. When we talk about honeymoons and futures and where we want to end up — I mean all of it. Every word.

the part that matters most

I am not a person who is good at saying the quiet things out loud. But you have always made it easier. So here it is, written down so you can come back to it whenever you need:


I love you. Not as a reflex or a habit, but as a choice I make every single day and would make a thousand times over. I love you in the calm moments and the chaotic ones. I love you when you're making me laugh and when you make me want to tear my hair out. I love you past all the ways I know how to say it.


You're going to do incredible things. I don't say that to fill space — I say it because I have watched you and I know. And I am going to be there for every single one of them, cheering the loudest, insufferably proud, completely yours.
