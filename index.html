what I want you to know

Shoko - you are chaotic and soft and sharp all at once, and somehow that combination is the most beautiful thing I've ever had the privilege of knowing up close. You say things that make me laugh until I can't breathe, and then in the same conversation you'll say something so genuinely tender that I don't know what to do with myself.


I've watched you on the hard days too. The ones where you're tired and it shows. I've seen how much you carry and how easy you make it look for everyone else. You deserve so much more gentleness than you give yourself.

"I want to make you happy." — and I mean it every single time.

That's not a line. It's not something I say to fill silence. It's the thought that sits behind everything — the reason I show up, the reason I stay, the reason we talk about honeymoon destinations like they're already decided. Because for me, they kind of are.


I don't know how to be quiet about the fact that I love you. I'm not going to try.
